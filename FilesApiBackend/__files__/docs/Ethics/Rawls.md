# Rawls

## Social Contract content

* hypothetical state of nature without society

* benefits derived from living in society

Contracts must be consent based and reciprocally beneficial

People don't deserve to be born rich or poor, gifted or stunted. So they shouldn't be favored by societal institutions for this reason. Rawls argues for equality based reciprocity: All goods are to be distributed equally unless unequal distribution is to everyone's benefit.

Rawls's ideas attempt to

* establish basic rights

* address social/economic inequalities

According to Rawls, meritocracy doesn't work because of the natural lottery

We should reward those based on merit but in a way that benefits those who lost out
